			BME Changes Log

V0.6
====

Ability to select your tileset (by directory)
FASA Standard size added
Better Save Files implemented - Two open modes - this Version 
	only, so people can convert their map files!
Mouse Paint feature - clicking on a hex once fills just that hex, but 
dragging the mouse across several hexes 'paints' all of them with
whatever you have active.
Show Position feature - highlights the current Hex.
Printing! At Last!!
